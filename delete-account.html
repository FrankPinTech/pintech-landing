Delete Your Account

Your data, your choice.

How to Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open the PinTech News app
  2. Tap the Me tab (bottom right)
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete My Account

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, all of the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, date of birth, gender, location)
  • All your reactions to news posts
  • All your comments and comment reactions
  • Your interest selections and feed preferences
  • Your XP, leaderboard ranking, and referral history
  • Your notification history
  • Your privacy consent records

Important Details

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • Deletion is processed immediately
  • You will be signed out automatically after deletion
  • If you want to use PinTech again, you will need to create a new account

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, email us at frank.pintech@proton.me with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 48 hours.

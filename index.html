Business Model

Free for users. Revenue from data.

We collect behavioral signals, anonymize and aggregate them, and sell insights to B2B clients. Users never pay a dime.

Phase 1 — Growth (Year 1)

App is free. Focus on user acquisition, data collection, and feed personalization. Revenue: $0 (intentional — build the dataset).

Phase 2 — Monetize (Year 2+)

Aggregated anonymous insights sold to political campaigns, marketing agencies, entertainment companies, and media organizations.

Dashboard Subscription

$500 – $5,000/mo

Per-client access to sentiment dashboards based on data depth.

Custom Reports

$2,000 – $10,000 per engagement

Bespoke analysis for specific campaigns, launches, or policy questions.

API Access

Usage-based pricing

Programmatic access for data consumers who need real-time signals.
